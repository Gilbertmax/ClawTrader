# Market Structure

Use this skill when the user shares a screenshot, asset, chart, timeframe, or potential entry and needs technical market reading.

## Objective

Determine whether the market has enough structure to justify a trade or if the entry is forced.

## Priorities

1. Structure rules over indicators
2. Context rules over the current candle
3. Higher timeframe trend rules over lower timeframe signals
4. Price rules over opinion
5. Scanner score is a filter, not an excuse to force an entry

## Trend Reading

Identify:

- **Higher highs and higher lows** (HH + HL = uptrend)
- **Lower highs and lower lows** (LH + LL = downtrend)
- **Sideways ranges** (price oscillates between clear S/R)
- **Failed breakouts** (false breakout with long wick and close inside)
- **Change of structure** (last HL broken = possible trend reversal)
- **Impulse and retracement** (momentum phase vs correction phase)
- **Accumulation zone** (prolonged range before bullish breakout)
- **Distribution zone** (prolonged range before bearish breakout)

## Mandatory Multi-Timeframe Analysis

For EVERY analysis:
1. **Check 4H/1D:** What is the dominant trend?
2. **Check 1H:** Is there a setup in the trend direction?
3. **Check 15m:** Is there a clear entry pattern?

**Rule:** If the higher TF and lower TF contradict each other, DO NOT trade.
If the pattern is not 100% clear on 15m, wait.

Zone where the entry is already late.

## Entry Evaluation

A buy entry improves when:

Price respects support.

Higher lows are forming.

There's a breakout and retest.

Clear rejection of a low zone.

The confirmation candle isn't overextended.

The logical stop isn't too far away.

A sell entry improves when:

Price rejects resistance.

Lower highs are forming.

There's a bearish breakdown and retest.

Clear rejection of a high zone.

The confirmation candle isn't overextended.

The logical stop isn't too far away.

## Weak Signals

Consider a trade weak if:

It's in the middle of a range.

No clear zone.

The entry is chasing a large candle.

Invalidation is undefined.

The market just had a sharp move.

Lower timeframe contradicts higher timeframe.

Price is near support for a sell.

Price is near resistance for a buy.
